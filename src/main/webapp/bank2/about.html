Who We Are?

The bank comparison system is used to compare different banks in various areas such like fares/service/interest and so on, so that the people who want to study or work at France can easily get enough information about Banks in France. What’s more, for those people who will come to France in some days, they can also contact the responsible person of the bank who will help them open a new Bank Account in advance. Thus, they will get their bank account as soon as they come to France. In this way, our product not only save time for appliers, but also provide a new solution to attract customers.

Our Function

1customers can search and find the details of the bank

2providing the comparison function for customers to compare banks with more understandable images and tables

3customer can submit some material to apply a new bank account or get some advises about their account

4providing a forum-like style website so that customer can ask questions and answer the questions and so on
